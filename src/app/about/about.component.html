ABOUT
ZRA

ZRA is a small business with over 20 years of experience in cybersecurity, risk management, program management, strategic planning, and law and policy.

ZRA has an experienced team of strategic thinkers with law and policy, computer science, business, audit, finance, and engineering backgrounds. ZRA's dynamic team provides the government and leading critical infrastructure companies with innovative approaches to address cybersecurity challenges.

ZRA is a trusted advisor to senior leaders and executives in both the public and private sectors. Our community of partners and customers include federal and state governments, Fortune 200 companies, venture capital firms, trade associations, research and development centers, and top universities.
