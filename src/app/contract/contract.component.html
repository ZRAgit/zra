ZRA's Contract Vehicles

ZRA actively manages our contract and contract vehicle portfolio to provide our customers with easy access to our capabilities. We work aggressively to participate in contract vehicles such as indefinite delivery and quantity (IDIQ) and blanket purchase agreements to provide ultimate flexibility for contracting officials. ZRA is also an experienced prime contractor and has teamed with numerous other small and large businesses to identify and win contracts and IDIQ across the Federal Civilian Government and the Department of Defense.
